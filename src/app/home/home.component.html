This is The Record Store Map - the best place to find record stores near you and around the world. If you see something is wrong, missing or not working - please get in contact.
We offer items with good prices and the latest titles at a discount, so we recommend that you do not miss the availability of the records of your favorite authors.
Discover our collection of signed and limited edition releases, as well as Recordstore exclusives you won't find anywhere else - including releases from Eric Clapton, Vinnie Moore, Metallica, Avenged Sevenfold, Joe Bonamasa, Sliplknot, Steve Lukather and loads more!
We also work with labels, artists and management year-round to come up with the releases that help make Record Store Day special. If you've got some thoughts on records you'd like to see on the List -- either special things that haven't been on vinyl, reissues you'd love to see on vinyl and/or CD, or ideas for other titles, please let us know below. No promises, but we'd love to add your thoughts and requests to the mix!
